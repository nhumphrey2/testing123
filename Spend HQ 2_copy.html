The SpendHQ Process

A SaaS Solution Combined with Data Optimization Services

Consolidation, Normalization, and Custom Categorization of Spend Data

Based on our 15+ year history of enabling spend visibility for leading companies, our innovative data approach incorporates machine learning and algorithm-based analytics built upon the $1.5T in spend and 100MM unique vendor records we’ve categorized over the years.

Humanware Component

SpendHQ procurement experts perform the necessary “human logic” with resources based in North America to ensure your data is truly categorized from a strategic sourcing perspective.

Procurement Partnership

SpendHQ’s sourcing experts work with your team to develop a custom spend taxonomy that reflects a true “sourcing-level” category structure.

Spend Visibility

Through our simple and easy-to-use interface, instantly identify untapped saving opportunities and create custom reports with multiple layers of analytics to view your spend the way you need to see it.

Post-Sourcing Analytics

SpendHQ is the only spend analytics provider that focuses on managing categories after sourcing.

Spend Diagnostic

SpendHQ performs recurring spend diagnostics, allowing you to go beyond your own data and receive free insights into sourcing and cost-optimization strategies
