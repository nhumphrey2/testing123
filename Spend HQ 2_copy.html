The SpendHQ Process

Consolidation, Normalization, and Custom Categorization of Spend Data

Humanware Component

SpendHQ procurement experts perform the necessary “human logic” with resources based in North America to ensure your data is truly categorized from a strategic sourcing perspective.

Procurement Partnership

SpendHQ’s sourcing experts work with your team to develop a custom spend taxonomy that reflects a true “sourcing-level” category structure.

Spend Visibility

Through our simple and easy-to-use interface, instantly identify untapped saving opportunities and create custom reports with multiple layers of analytics to view your spend the way you need to see it.
