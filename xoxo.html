My SEO Skills

A high-ranking organic search score(without paying for Google Ads) requires many technical skills. A high SEO ranking is a marathon; not a sprint. Also, a great top level domain (TLD), is crucial.

Try to keep your unique domain name under 15 characters long, and research your competitors. I understand how to use: Google's Data highlighter tool, creating your brand using Google+, Google Analytics, JSON-LD & Microdata Schema markup, structured data testing, best HTML5 practices, among others.

What You Need For a Great SEO Score

  • Google Webmaster Tools - Submit both www.neilkhumphrey.com and neilkhumphrey.com(This domain as an example), to Google Search console.Choose your preferred domain with or without the "www." in front of your URL.
  • Https Secured Domain - Google just announced in 2017, that using a secure "https" domain will increase your position among unsecured "http" domains.
  • Schema.org - Your website should have Microdata and JSON-LD markup code implemented in your HTML.
  • XML Sitemap - Submit your site's XML sitemap with using Google Webmaster Tools. This allows Google to crawl your website when pages are updated.
  • Verify Your Website - Verify your site with Google, Bing, and Yandex.
